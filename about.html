Who We Are

About WMC Worldwide Medical Center

At WMC Worldwide Medical Center we believe healthcare should go beyond just treating illness but also focus on the overall wellbeing of the individual. We strive to make people healthy in mind, body and spirit, improve their quality of life, develop them as productive members of society; and create healthier communities for everyone.

As a full-service medical center, we use state-of-the-art technology combined with personal care to provide complete health care solutions to our clients. Our medical staff consists of Physicians, Surgeons, Rehabilitation Specialists and other allied health professionals who provide client-centered care based on the principles of prevention, education and community wellness. At WMC we provide a complete continuum of care from prevention to recovery. Our mission is to support our clients' journey to becoming healthy and happy.

  • Innovation & Quality with comprehensive medical advisory team
  • FDA-Accepted, State of the Art Systems and technology
  • Extremely Skillful & Committed Team with continuous training

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by purpose and commitment to excellence in healthcare.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an exceptional level of service that places each individual at the centre of the health care experience. With the utmost integrity, compassion, and innovation, we will provide individuals with the equipment needed to take charge of their own health through access to high-quality clinical services, preventative medical services and ongoing support.

Our Vision

We see a future where a high standard of health care is not something that only a select few will have, but a right that all people will have access to worldwide. In the future, we will be recognized as a trusted medical organisation, and an innovator in both research and in providing patient-centered care, to demonstrate the promise of medical excellence.

Why Choose WMC?

Excellence in care, innovation in treatment, and commitment to your wellbeing.

Innovation & Quality

WMC has a comprehensive medical and aesthetic advisory team to stay current with new technologies and developments in science. We provide the latest methods and instruments to give you the best, safest, and most effective treatments.

DHA-Approved, State of the Art Systems

We use only the highest-quality devices approved by the DHA and state-of-the-art systems to ensure you receive an exceptional level of patient care based on clinical excellence.

Extremely Skillful & Committed Team

Our professionals are more than just highly trained; they are extremely committed and very passionate about providing their patients with the best level of service possible. All of our staff are continually required to receive ongoing training as part of our company culture for continuous learning.

Personalized Care

We understand that all of our patients have different medical and aesthetic needs. At WMC we take time to listen carefully to your individual goals, customize your treatment plan accordingly, and ensure you receive appropriate follow up and after care.
